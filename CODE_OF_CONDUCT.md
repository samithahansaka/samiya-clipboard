# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We're building @samiya/clipboard in the open, and we want everyone to feel welcome here.

Whether you're opening your first issue, submitting a PR, or just asking a question — you belong here. We're committed to providing a friendly, safe, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age, body size, or physical appearance
- Disability (visible or invisible)
- Ethnicity, nationality, or race
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience or education
- Personal background or beliefs
- Religion or lack thereof
- Sexual identity and orientation

## What We Expect

**Be kind.** That's really it. But here are some specifics:

### Positive Behaviors

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy to others
- Help newcomers feel welcome
- Give credit where it's due

### Unacceptable Behaviors

- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted attention
- Trolling, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Dismissing or attacking someone's identity or background
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking fair corrective action when needed
- Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that violate this code
- Banning contributors (temporarily or permanently) for harmful behavior

## Where This Applies

This Code of Conduct applies:

- In all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, code reviews)
- In public spaces when representing the project
- When using official project accounts or communications

## Reporting Issues

See something? Say something.

**Report violations to:** [Create a private issue](https://github.com/samithahansaka/samiya-clipboard/issues/new) with the title "Code of Conduct Report" or email the maintainer directly.

All reports will be:
- Reviewed promptly and confidentially
- Investigated fairly
- Responded to appropriately

We will not retaliate against anyone who reports a concern in good faith.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

1. **Warning** — Private notice about the violation
2. **Temporary Ban** — Temporary exclusion from project spaces
3. **Permanent Ban** — Permanent exclusion from the community

The response will match the severity and context of the violation.
