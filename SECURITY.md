## Reporting a Vulnerability

Found a security issue? We take it seriously.

**Please DO NOT open a public issue.**

Instead:

1. Email the details to **samithahansaka** (via [GitHub profile](https://github.com/samithahansaka))
2. Or open a [private security advisory](https://github.com/samithahansaka/samiya-clipboard/security/advisories/new)

### What to Include

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if any)

### What to Expect

- **Response:** Within 48 hours
- **Update:** We'll keep you informed of progress
- **Credit:** We'll acknowledge your contribution (unless you prefer anonymity)

## Scope

This library is a client-side React hook with zero dependencies. The attack surface is minimal, but we still appreciate reports about:

- XSS vulnerabilities
- Clipboard data exposure
- Any unexpected behavior that could be exploited
